History


CKHS Black Shirts Rugby Team, situated in the heart of Taiwan, has earned an esteemed reputation as one of the most formidable high school rugby teams in the country. Clad in their distinctive black jerseys, they exude an aura of power and determination that strikes fear into their opponents on the field. Renowned for their unwavering passion, unwavering courage, and relentless teamwork, the CKHS rugby team has become synonymous with the very essence of the sport.

The CKHS players are no strangers to hard work and dedication, which has shaped them into exceptional athletes. Hours of training, both on and off the field, have honed their skills to perfection, allowing them to execute precise and robust tactics during matches. The team's commitment to physicality and solid tackling has earned them the admiration of rugby enthusiasts across Taiwan and beyond.

Moreover, the CKHS Black Shirts are not just a group of individual talents; they are a cohesive unit that operates as one on the rugby pitch. This unity and camaraderie translate into seamless teamwork, enabling them to anticipate each other's moves and make split-second decisions that turn the tide in their favor. Their combined strength, both in body and spirit, has proven to be an unstoppable force in Taiwan Rugby.

Beyond their dominance on the field, the CKHS rugby team is admired for their sportsmanship and humility. Despite their impressive track record, they remain grounded and always strive to improve. Their commitment to fair play and respect for opponents has earned them admiration from rivals and fans alike.

The CKHS rugby program is not just about winning matches; it's about fostering a deep love for the sport and nurturing the next generation of rugby enthusiasts. Their coaches and mentors play a pivotal role in instilling discipline, values, and a sense of responsibility in each player. As a result, the team has become a tight-knit family that supports and encourages one another both on and off the field.

With each match they play, the CKHS Black Shirts Rugby Team leaves an indelible mark on the sport of rugby in Taiwan. Their legacy extends beyond trophies and championships; it is about the enduring spirit of the game and the profound impact it has on the lives of these young athletes. As they continue to inspire and forge their path to greatness, the CKHS rugby team remains a beacon of excellence, passion, and teamwork in the world of Taiwan Rugby.
